## Hello, World

Welcome to my personal blog. This is a space where I'll share my thoughts on design, code, and the craft of building things for the web. If you've landed here, you probably know me already — but if not, hello. I'm glad you're here.

I've been meaning to start writing publicly for a while. The usual excuses applied: not enough time, nothing interesting to say, who would even read it. None of those were really true, so here we are.

## What to expect

I'll be writing about a few things:

- **Design** — interface design, typography, the small decisions that shape how things feel
- **Code** — front-end patterns, tools, whatever I'm learning or building at the time
- **Process** — how I think about creative work, how I stay focused, how I avoid overcomplicating things

> I write to think, not to perform. Expect unfinished ideas alongside more polished essays.

## A note on cadence

I won't commit to a posting schedule. I'll write when I have something worth saying. Quality over cadence — I'd rather publish one good thing a month than five forgettable ones a week.

If you'd like to follow along, there's an RSS feed linked in the footer.
